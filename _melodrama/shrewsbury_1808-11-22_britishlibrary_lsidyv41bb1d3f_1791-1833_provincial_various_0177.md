- |-
  THE CELEBRATED PLAY, OF
  PIZARRO,
  And the GRAND DRAMATIC ROMANCE of
  BLUE BEARD.

  To meet the Wishes of the Public, the Theatre will present ON THIS EVENING, the above two very favourite Performances : being assuredly the only Time on which there can be a Representation of either of those much admired Pieces.
- |-
  On THURSDAY, the 14th
  The Celebrated Mr. Richer,
  Who returns here on that Day in his Way to Ireland, will have the Honour to present several new, additional, and very extraordinary Exploits on THE TIGHT ROPE. He will also perform the Part of FRANCISCO, in the admired MELODRAMA of "THE TALE OF MYSTERY," as played by him in London ;- and which Piece, together with his Performances, will be represented on that Evening, after (first Time this Season) the very favourite Comedy of “THE SCHOOL FOR SCANDAL."

  And, on FRIDAY EVENING, the 26th, he will display very extraordinary Performances, after a favourite Comedy ; to which will be added (for the only Time) the celebrated Pantomime of “ DON JUAN; or, THE LIBERTINE DESTROYED." The Part of DON JUAN, by Mr. RICHER, being for his Benefit, and assuredly THE LAST NIGHT of his appearing.
